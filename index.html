DOrSU joins CSC Celebration;conducts free Pap smear, flu vax.

BY PIO ~ PUBLISHED SEPTEMBER 26, 2022 ~ UPDATED SEPTEMBER 26,2022

The Davao Oriental State University offered free flu vaccines to all employees and free Pap smear to women as part of the Civil Service Commission (CSC) Month celebration this September 2022. DOrSU President Dr. Roy G. Ponce stated that this initiative of the Human Resource Management Office, carried out in collaboration with the University’s Health Services Unit and City Health Office (Mati), is to help employees maintain their wellness while engaging in daily transactions of the university. In lieu of the annual check-up, free flu vaccination prevents employees from seasonal influenza viruses that cause illness, allowing them to continue providing high-quality services. Pap smear, on the other hand, helps women’s early detection of cervical cancer for a greater chance of a cure. DOrSU is also conducting a Zumba dance workout this month every Thursday since September 8, 2022.

DOrSU, DILG sign MoA on LGPRC Operationalization

BY PIO ~ PUBLISHED APRIL 3,2022 ~ UPDATED APRIL 7,2022

DOrSU President Roy G. Ponce signed a Memorandum of Agreement (MoA) with the Department of Interior and Local Government (DILG), represented by Provincial Director Orle A. Cabaobao, on the organization of the Multi-Stakeholders Advisory Committee for the Operationalization of the Local Governance Provincial Resource Center (LGPRC) of Davao Oriental, last April 1, 2022, at the University Boardroom. As a partner, DOrSU will provide space for the resource materials, collaborate with DILG – Davao Oriental in the planning, implementation, monitoring, and evaluation of the LGPRC’s programs, projects, and activities, provide technical expertise and assistance on the research component, intervention programs implementation, and advocacy development, facilitate the conduct of intervention programs/training and seminars, among others, in coordination with the Multi- stakeholders Advisory Committee MSAC members, develop information and data, and provide technical expertise and assistance on the research component, intervention program implementation, and advocacy development. President Ponce and PD Cabaobao then cut the ribbon to formally launch the Local Governance Provincial Resource Center located on the second floor of the University Library. LGPRC will be integrated in the Data Science Center management of DOrSU.
